Cyber Crime Units / Police Stations / Cells in India

The victim of cyber crime can approach a local police station whichever near to him (residence) and seek help by getting a case registered under Information Technology Act, 2000 and also under other relevant statues like Indian Penal code.

The victim of cyber crime can also approach directly Cyber Crime Units / Police Stations / cells that are operational Indian States and UTs.
