Cyber Crime Units in India

The victims of cyber crime can approach the local police station which ever nearer to him and seek help by getting a case registered under Information Technology Act and also under other relevant statues like Indian Penal code. A police officer of the rank of Inspector or above are empowered to investigate offences under Information Technology Act

The victims can also approach directly Cyber Crime Units / Police Stations that are setup in every state in India
